About Angel's Burger

Karinderya started as a small family table where simple, delicious burgers brought people together. We combine classic techniques with bold flavors so every bite tastes familiar yet exciting.

Our Ingredients

We source fresh produce and use high-quality beef blends. Cheese is melted to perfection, and buns are toasted for that ideal texture.

Our Values

Affordable comfort food, fast friendly service, and food that feels like home.

Community

We support local suppliers and welcome everyone — students, families, and late-night burger lovers.
